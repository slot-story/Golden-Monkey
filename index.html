Golden Monkey Overview

The Golden Monkey is a popular character of Chinese literature. This slot is inspired by the local Chinese hero Sun Wu Kong and his interactions with monsters, enemies, and demons during his travel to the Western countries. This Space Gaming gambling machine is a very commonly played one at all the licensed casinos online. How to Play?

Once the user has the Golden Monkey slot loaded on the screen, he/she needs to choose the number of lines, the coin size and the bets.

  • Use the ‘+/-’ option under the denomination option to choose the coin size that ranges from 0.01 to 1.00.
  • Adjust the number of lines that you want to wager on by pressing the + or the – under the respective option.
  • Choose the bet number that ranges from 1 to 5 under the respective option.
  • After this, press the spin option to start the reels.
